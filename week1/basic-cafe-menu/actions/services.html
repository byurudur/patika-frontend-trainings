Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi Menu

Turkish Coffee Specialties:

  • Traditional Turkish Coffee - A timeless classic brewed to perfection.
  • Kurukahveci Mehmet Efendi Blend - Our signature blend, honoring our founder.
  • Sade (Plain) - Pure and unadorned, highlighting the essence of Turkish coffee.
  • Orta Şekerli (Medium Sweet) - Balanced sweetness for a delightful experience.
  • Şekerli (Sweet) - Indulgent sweetness, perfect for those with a sweet tooth.

Specialty Beverages:

  • Turkish Tea - A refreshing complement to our coffee offerings.
  • Salep - A warm, comforting drink made from orchid tubers and spices.
  • Ayran - A traditional Turkish yogurt drink, cool and refreshing.

Pastries and Sweets:

  • Baklava - Layers of flaky pastry filled with nuts and sweetened with syrup.
  • Simit - Sesame-coated bread rings, a beloved Turkish snack.
  • Revani - Semolina sponge cake soaked in syrup, a delightful dessert.

Desserts:

  • Künefe - Cheese pastry soaked in sweet syrup, served warm.
  • Tavuk Göğsü - Milk pudding with shredded chicken, a unique Turkish delicacy.
  • Şekerpare - Semolina cookies soaked in syrup, topped with almonds.

Snacks:

  • Çiğ Köfte - Spicy bulgur and raw meatballs, a flavorful Turkish snack.
  • Kumpir - Baked potato stuffed with various toppings, a hearty choice.

Join us at Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi to experience the authentic flavors of Turkish coffee and cuisine, lovingly prepared to delight your senses and honor our heritage.
