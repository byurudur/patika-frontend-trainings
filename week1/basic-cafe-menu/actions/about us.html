About Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi

Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi traces its origins back to 1882 in the Tokat province of the Ottoman Empire, where Küpçü Mehmet began selling coffee. What started as a humble venture selling coffee beans soon grew into a beloved institution renowned for its quality and tradition.

Over the years, Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi has evolved into a symbol of Turkish coffee culture, embodying a commitment to excellence and authenticity. Our journey from a small coffee shop to a respected name in the industry is a testament to our dedication to preserving the art of coffee making.

At Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi, we continue to uphold the legacy of Küpçü Mehmet by meticulously selecting the finest coffee beans and adhering to time-honored brewing techniques. Each cup of coffee served at our café reflects our passion for delivering an exceptional sensory experience rooted in Turkish coffee tradition.

As we look towards the future, we remain committed to expanding our reach while staying true to our heritage. Küpçüoğlu Kahvecisi invites you to savor the essence of Turkish coffee culture, where every cup tells a story of craftsmanship and heritage.
